TouchMed

TouchMed is India's first marketplace B2B application for healthcare professionals. TouchMed believes that the Indian doctors and pharmacists need better options while buying medicines.

Play Store

Registration

This application is only for healthcare professionals. Only certified doctors and pharmacists can access this application. For registration, kindly submit the required details on the application. The details shall be verified and account shall be activated within 24 working hours.

Features

  • TouchMed is the first B2B marketplace application for healthcare professionals.
  • TouchMed delivers medicines directly from the pharmaceutical companies to the customers, thus overcoming the middle suppliers. This ensures top quality medicines are supplied at rates better than even generics in the market.
  • TouchMed houses medicines only from Wintech Pharmaceuticals & Zenova Therapeutics.
  • These companies have served more than 40 million patients worldwide. So quality of the medicines provided on the application is unparalleled.
  • At the same time, TouchMed is the only application in India providing medicines directly to healthcare professionals from pharmaceutical companies at such a large scale.
  • So you get top quality products from TouchMed at never before pricing

Products

TouchMed has a wide range of products. All products are manufactured under strict quality adherening systems. The range of products is wide. TouchMed has Analgesics, Anti-Biotics, Anti-Alergics, Anti-Fungals, Anti-Hpypertensives, Anti-Malarials, Calcium Preparations, Cough & Cold Preparations, GI Preparations, Haematinic Preparations, Steroids, Anti-Acne Preparations, MultiVitamins, Anti-Pyretics, etc. These preparations are available in various formats - Orals, Liquids, Injectables, Ointments etc.
