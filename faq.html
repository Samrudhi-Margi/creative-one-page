My Account & My Order

  1. How do I place my order?
    2.  To place your order, follow these steps :
    1. Browse through our wide catalog of medicines until you find something you require, fill in the required quantity and click on the ‘Add to Cart’ button (you are eligible to add multiple medicines on your cart).
    2. ‘In My cart’, you can either choose to continue shopping or confirm the order.
    3. Once the cart is all set, proceed to checkout, select the desired payment process and confirm your order.

  2. How will I receive my Order?
    3.  We will deliver your product on your mentioned shipping address. We deliver the products directly to your doorstep. All orders will be delivered in fully sealed packages to protect your goods and ensure that they reach you in perfect condition.

  3. How will I get confirmation that my Order is placed successfully?
    4.  We'll send an SMS with your order value information to confirm.

  4. I tried placing my order using my debit card/credit card/Net Banking. The order was not successful but my money got deducted. What really happened with my money?
    5.  1. Please check your bank/credit card account to first ensure if your account has been debited. If your account has been debited after a payment failure, it is normally rolled back by banks within 7 business days.
    2. For assistance you can write to us at info@touchmed.in, we will be happy to help you out.

  5. How do I cancel my order?
    6.  For prepaid orders, please contact our Customer Care at info@touchmed.in to cancel the order and refund your payment. The refund amount will be refunded to your account, minus the payment charges.
    If the order is shipped already, unfortunately we wouldn’t be able to cancel it. In case you desire any changes or edits in your order, please write to us at info@touchmed.in Our Customer Care representatives will help you out with customized solutions wherever possible.

  6. What is the return/refund policy of TouchMed?
    7.  At TouchMed, we do our best to ensure that you are completely satisfied with our products. We understand that returns may be necessary in some cases. And we are happy to offer you peace of mind when you are not fully satisfied with the purchase and want to return back the products to us. You may return the ordered item(s), or any item(s) included in the order, within 7 days of the arrival of your order.
    You may receive a full refund based on the conditions listed below:

    Please note: TouchMed's ‘Returns Policy’ may vary depending on the specific nature of the products.
    Refunds Possible If the Item(s) You Received:
    • is different from the ones you ordered;
    • are lost or damaged during transit;
    • is past its expiry date;
    • is a defective item.

    Returns NOT Eligible If the Item(s) You Ordered:
    • is without original packing/accessories.
    • is used or tampered with.

Payment Mode

  1. Are there any COD charges?
    2.  No, there is no COD charge.

  2. Is there any minimum order value?
    3.  No, there is no minimum order value. But there is a delivery charge of Rs. 99 on any order below Rs. 1000. Above Rs. 1000 order value, the delivery charge is borne by TouchMed.

  3. Is it safe to shop online if I make payment using net banking, Debit card or Credit Card?
    4.  All Credit Card, Debit Card and net banking transactions are processed over a secure encrypted connection. We have tied up with a renowned secure payment company to ensure that your payments are secure.

  4. Are there any hidden charges?
    5.  There are absolutely no hidden charges. You pay only the amount that you see in your order summary.

  5. What should I do if my payment fails?
    6.  In case of a payment failure, please retry ensuring :
    1. Information passed on to payment gateway is accurate i.e. account details, billing address, password (for net banking) and that your Internet connection is not disrupted in the process.
    2. If your account has been debited after a payment failure, it is normally rolled back to your bank account within 7 business days For any further clarification, you can email us on info@touchmed.in.

  6. What is the delivery time period?
    7.  Standard delivery orders will be delivered within 3 working days, wherein the day of order is considered as 'day 0'. It should be noted that 'Sundays' and all 'national & regional' holidays shall be considered as non-working days.

  7. Do you apply any shipping charges?
    8.  Touchmed provides Free Shipping for order above Rs 999. There will be a shipping charge of Rs 99 on orders of Rs. 999 or below.

  8. Will I automatically receive marketing emails from you after registration?
    9.  If you register with us, you are inevitably getting subscribed to our newsletter. If you don't wish to receive our newsletter, you can click on the unsubscribe link in the newsletter.

  9. What to do if I forgot my password?
    10.  Just click on 'Forgot Password' option and we will send it to you unately you cannot add any more products to your order. You can however, place a new order.

Medicine Policy

  1. Can I register a patient on the application so that he gets the medicines directly?
    2.  No. TouchMed does not provide medicines directly to any patient. It is a service only for registered medical professionals. No medicine shall be delivered directly to any patient.

  2. Does TouchMed recommend substitution of the medicines prescribed by doctors?
    3.  No. TouchMed does not recommend any substitution of any prescription. The Terms & Conditions have clearly chalked the medication policy of TouchMed. We recommend the user to go through it.

  3. Can the medicines be delivered at my residential address?
    4.  No, TouchMed shall not deliver any product at your residential address. The medicines can only be delivered at a 'clinic' of a doctor or a medical store.

  4. Why is the application not available on the Google App Store?
    5.  The application is a closed application and has not been put up on the Google Play Store. TouchMed wants only registered Medical Professionals to have link for the application. So we are sending the download link to all interested customers directly.
